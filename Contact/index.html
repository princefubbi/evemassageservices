Common questions

How do I book a session? ▼
The easiest way is to message us on WhatsApp at 09055853040. You can also use our Pricing & Booking page on the website. We'll confirm your booking and send a reminder before your session.
Do you offer home service? ▼
Yes — home service is available across Akwa Ibom State. We bring everything needed for your session. Transport fees may apply for locations outside Eket and Uyo. Contact us to confirm for your specific area.
What should I do before my session? ▼
Avoid heavy meals 1–2 hours before. Stay hydrated. Inform us of any medical conditions, injuries, or medications before your session. Arrive or be ready 5–10 minutes early to allow time to settle.
What are your payment methods? ▼
We currently accept cash and bank transfer. Payment is made at the time of service. For package bookings a deposit may be required to confirm the session. Contact us for bank details when booking.
Can I cancel or reschedule? ▼
Yes — cancellations made 24 hours or more before your session have no charge. Cancellations with less than 24 hours notice may incur a 50% fee. See our full cancellation policy in our Terms & Conditions.
Do you offer corporate packages? ▼
Yes — we offer corporate wellness packages for businesses and organisations in Akwa Ibom. These include regular on-site sessions and staff wellness programmes. Email us at evemassageservices@gmail.com to discuss.
I have a medical condition — can I still book? ▼
In many cases yes — but please disclose your condition when booking so we can advise on the most suitable service. We specialise in clinical therapies including stroke recovery and diabetes support. Some conditions may require medical clearance first.
Are your therapists qualified? ▼
Yes — all Eve Massage Services therapists are trained and certified in their respective disciplines. We maintain professional standards for all services including our specialist clinical therapies.
